Play to Win!

Instructions

Our experiment consists of 4 rounds of betting. In each round, you are endowed with 100 units to bet. You can bet as much or as little of the 100 units as you want. You have a ⅓ chance of making 2 ½ times the money you bet and you have ⅔ chance of losing all your money. You will see the results of your bets once every two rounds. We will add up your total units at the end and we will divide the $10 reward among the people who end with a net higher amount. ALSO please write down the amount you bet each round and the displayed total units you end with after each round on the provided piece of paper
